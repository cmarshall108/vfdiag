# 13 — Warranty & Support (US, 2024 MY)

## Headline Warranty Coverage

| Coverage | Term |
|----------|------|
| **New vehicle limited warranty** | **10 years / 125,000 miles** (industry-leading at launch) |
| **High-voltage battery warranty** | **10 years / unlimited miles**, replacement if SoH drops below **70 %** |
| **Powertrain (motors, reduction gearboxes, PE)** | Included in 10/125k bumper-to-bumper |
| **Roadside assistance** | **10 years / unlimited miles**, 24/7 |
| **Corrosion (perforation)** | Per VinFast schedule |
| **Adjustment (alignment, balance)** | Typically 12 mo / 12,000 mi |

> The 10-year coverage was a key differentiator at launch. It is **transferable** to subsequent owners (verify limits in the current Warranty Booklet).

## Battery Subscription (legacy)

Early 2023 deliveries used a **leased-battery** model (vehicle bought, battery rented monthly, ~$169–$219/mo depending on annual mileage tier). Through the 2024 MY, VinFast transitioned the US program toward **battery-included pricing**. Existing subscribers retained their original terms.

## Service Network

- VinFast-owned showrooms/service centers in CA, NY, FL, TX, NV, MA, NJ, CT (expanding).
- **Mobile service** for non-HV work (12 V battery, software, tires).
- Third-party body-shop network certified by VinFast for collision repair.

## Roadside Assistance

- Phone: 1-877-NOW-VINFAST (US) — verify current number on the VinFast app.
- Services: towing (to nearest VinFast service center), flat-tire (mobility kit assist), lockout, dead 12 V jump.
- **No EV-rated towing equipment guarantee at every event** — request a flatbed; the VF 8 should always be **flat-bedded**, never dolly-towed (AWD permanent).

## OTA & Software Support

- All MY 2024 VF 8s receive ongoing OTA updates for ECUs and infotainment.
- Update history and changelogs accessible via *Settings → Software Updates → History*.

## Cybersecurity & Privacy

- TBOX collects telematics (location, drive events, charge events) — opt-out tiers in *Settings → Privacy*.
- ADAS recordings: certain markets retain pre-event footage for crash investigation; refer to local privacy notice.
