# 01 — Overview, History & Trims

## Model Lineage

| Codename | Year | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| VF e35 | 2021 (concept) | Original CES 2021 unveil |
| VF 32 | 2021 | Internal development name |
| **VF 8** | 2022– | Production name (renamed Jan 2022) |

- **Manufacturer:** VinFast Trading & Production LLC (Vingroup)
- **Production start:** Vietnam, Sep 10, 2022 (first 100 customer deliveries)
- **US launch:** First California deliveries March 1, 2023 ("City Edition")
- **2024 MY:** Revised trim lineup, EPA-rerated range, software updates addressing early reviewer complaints. North Carolina assembly (Chatham County) was deferred — originally slated for 2024, now scheduled for ~2028.

## 2024 US Trim Lineup

The 2024 model year simplified the lineup to two trims, both **dual-motor AWD** with the extended-range pack:

| Trim | MSRP (US, 2024) | Motors | Power | EPA Range |
|------|-----------------|--------|-------|-----------|
| **Eco** | ≈ $46,000 | Dual PMSM | 348 hp / 368 lb-ft | ~207 mi |
| **Plus** | ≈ $49,000 | Dual PMSM | 402 hp / 457 lb-ft | ~191 mi |

VinFast initially offered a **battery subscription** (vehicle priced separately from a leased battery, ~$169–$219/month) but transitioned the US market to **battery-included pricing** during the 2024 MY.

## Body & Design

- **Body style:** 5-door, 5-passenger crossover SUV
- **Class:** D-segment (mid-size)
- **Exterior design:** Pininfarina, Italy
- **Interior:** "Two-tone" dash, center-tunnel drive-mode switches, **15.6" central touchscreen**, **head-up display (HUD)** replacing traditional gauge cluster
- **Notable feature:** ISO-mounted "Smart Services" infotainment with OTA updates
